## Customer Review Analysis

## Description

In this project, I analyzed customer review data from over 300 hotels across Europe using data sourced from Booking.com. I cleaned and prepared the dataset to identify key drivers of customer satisfaction and retention.

I developed a sentiment scoring approach to quantify customer feedback and used Power BI to visualize insights through bar charts and geographic (bubble map) visualizations. These visuals highlighted patterns in satisfaction across different locations and specific tags. The final deliverable was an interactive dashboard that allows users to explore trends and better understand factors influencing customer experience.

## Relationship to BCIS Learning Goal
This project demonstrates the application of business, statistical, modeling, and computer science principles to solve a real-world business problem. I identified the need to better understand customer satisfaction in the hospitality industry and developed a data driven approach to uncover actionable insights.

Using data analytics tools, I worked with both structured and unstructured data by cleaning text based reviews, creating sentiment scores, and organizing the data into a usable format. I then designed and built an interactive dashboard in Power BI to communicate findings effectively and support decision making.
