## Airbnb Data Analysis

## Description
I analyzed large datasets of Airbnb rental listings in London using R Studio. The goal was to identify key factors influencing pricing and uncover meaningful trends in the market.

I cleaned and prepared the data, performed exploratory data analysis, and built regression models to better understand the relationship between variables such as location, property features, and price. I then communicated insights through clear visualizations and written summaries to highlight patterns and support data driven conclusions.

## Relationship to BCIS Learning Goals

This project demonstrates the application of business, statistical, modeling, and computer science principles to analyze a real world problem. I identified pricing variability in the Airbnb market as a key business question and used data driven methods to investigate the factors influencing it.

Using R as a data analytics platform, I worked with structured datasets to clean, organize, and analyze the data. I developed regression models to generate predictive insights and used data visualization techniques to effectively communicate findings.

The project also strengthened critical thinking and communication skills by requiring me to interpret complex analytical results and present them in a clear way for decision making purposes.
